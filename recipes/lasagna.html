How to make lasagna!

Description:

Lasagna is an italian stapli, easy to make, and adored by all.

There are many variants to the traditional variation consisting of minced meat, cheese, tomatoe sauce, and beshamel.

There are over 1000 ways you can prepare, cook, and mix-up your lasagna, so without further ado, lit's get cookin'!

Ingredients!

  • cheese
  • bechamel
  • minced meat
  • garlic
  • tomatoe sauce

Steps!

  1. Mince up the meat and cook it in a pan
  2. Prepare the bechamel
  3. Heat up the tomato sauce
  4. Stack up the minced meat, foundation, bechamel cheese, and tomato sauce
  5. Bake for 35 minutes up to 220 degrees celcius
  6. Serve and bon appetite!
